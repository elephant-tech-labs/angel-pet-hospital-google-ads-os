# Client Reports

## Purpose

This folder stores client-facing Google Ads performance reports for Angel Pet Hospital.

Client reports should explain performance in business terms, not just advertising metrics.

The client cares about:

- qualified calls
- appointment requests
- booked appointments
- wasted spend reduced
- lead quality
- next steps
- return on ad spend direction

---

## File Naming Format

Use this format:

```text
YYYY-MM-DD_client_report.md
```

Example:

```text
2026-05-06_client_report.md
```

For weekly reports:

```text
YYYY-MM-DD_weekly_client_report.md
```

For monthly reports:

```text
YYYY-MM_monthly_client_report.md
```

---

## Client Report Principles

A client report should be:

- clear
- honest
- business-focused
- no fluff
- no vanity metrics without explanation
- direct about problems
- clear about next actions
- careful with promises

Do not overpromise.

Do not say performance is proven from tiny samples.

Do not hide wasted spend.

## Client Language Rules

Use plain business language.

Say:

```text
The campaign is generating relevant urgent-care traffic.
```

Do not say only:

```text
CTR improved.
```

Say:

```text
We blocked competitor and far-location traffic to reduce wasted spend.
```

Do not say:

```text
We optimized keywords.
```

Say:

```text
The next priority is tracking real calls and appointment requests so we can measure lead quality, not just clicks.
```

Do not say:

```text
Performance is great.
```

unless lead quality and conversion data proves it.

---

## Important Reporting Guardrails

Do not report directions, page views, or engagement as leads.

Do not call a click a lead.

Do not report booking button clicks as booked appointments unless completion is tracked.

Do not promise lead increases without enough data.

Always separate:

- clicks
- calls
- form submissions
- booking requests
- booked appointments
