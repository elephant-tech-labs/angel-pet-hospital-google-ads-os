- Edmonds
- call-first action

Only use if operationally accurate:

```text

Emergency Pet Care
```

24/7 Emergency Vet
Always Open

Before using or editing urgent campaign ads, check:
- No 24/7 claim unless confirmed.
- No guaranteed same-day appointment claim unless confirmed.
- No emergency surgery claim unless confirmed.
- Final URL must point to the urgent landing page.
- Phone CTA must use the correct number: (425) 673-0700.
- Copy should prioritize calls over booking for urgent traffic.
