# Negatives - General Vet Campaign

## Campaign

Search - General Vet - Edmonds

## Purpose

This file defines negative keyword strategy for the General Vet campaign.

The goal is to protect budget and keep General Vet traffic separate from:

- urgent/emergency intent
- brand intent
- dental intent
- surgery intent
- shelter/adoption intent
- grooming/boarding intent
- jobs/career/training intent
- far-location intent
- competitor intent

---

## Core Negative Strategy

The General Vet campaign must use:

```text
Global Vet Waste Negatives
```

plus campaign-specific negatives.

This campaign should not inherit messy traffic from old Campaign #1.

---

## Campaign-Specific Urgent Negatives

These keep urgent and emergency searches in the urgent campaign.

Add these as phrase negatives to the General Vet campaign:

```text
"urgent vet"
"urgent care vet"
"emergency vet"
"emergency animal hospital"
"pet emergency"
"vet open now"
"open now"
"same day vet"
```

### Important Note

Use these carefully.

Do not block:

```text
sick pet
sick dog
sick cat
```

at launch, because General Vet can handle non-emergency sick pet visits.

## Price-Sensitive Negatives

For normal General Vet campaigns, use:

```text
free
cheap
low cost

```

Watch before using:

```text

```

Reason:

These may be useful in approved promotional campaigns, but not in the standard General Vet campaign unless the clinic approves an offer.

## Negative Match Type Rules

### Broad Negatives

Use when the word is clearly bad in most contexts.

Examples:

```text
adoption
shelter
jobs
grooming
boarding
wildlife
zoo
```

### Phrase Negatives

Use when the phrase is bad but one word alone may be useful.

Examples:

```text
"animal shelter"
"pet adoption"
"vet jobs"
"low cost vet"
"emergency vet"
"urgent vet"
```

### Exact Negatives

Use when one exact query is bad but nearby variations may be useful.

Examples:

```text
[blue pearl kirkland]
[vet queen anne]
[bothell pet hospital]
```

Before campaign launch, confirm:

- Global Vet Waste Negatives are applied

- far-location negatives are reviewed
- no negatives accidentally block core general-vet intent

---

## Final Rule

The General Vet campaign should produce local appointment demand.

It should not become a catch-all campaign for every animal-related search.
