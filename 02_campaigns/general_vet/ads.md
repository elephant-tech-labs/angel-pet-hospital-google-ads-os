# Ads - General Vet Campaign

## Campaign

Search - General Vet - Edmonds

## Purpose

This file defines the approved ad copy strategy for the General Vet campaign.

The campaign should attract local pet owners looking for a regular veterinarian, animal hospital, dog vet, cat vet, or general pet care appointment.

This ad copy should feel:

- local
- trustworthy
- calm
- professional
- booking-focused
- clear

It should not feel like an emergency-only campaign.

---

## Campaign Positioning

Core positioning:

```text
Trusted Vet Care in Edmonds
```

Supporting message:

```text
Book compassionate veterinary care for dogs and cats with Angel Pet Hospital.
```

## Headlines To Avoid In General Vet Campaign

Avoid emergency-focused headlines here because those belong in the urgent campaign.

```text
Emergency Vet Near You
Emergency Pet Care
Urgent Vet Care Today
Pet Emergency Near Me
Vet Open Now
Same-Day Emergency Care
```

Reason:

These may steal urgent intent from the urgent campaign and confuse campaign reporting.

---

## Unsafe Claims To Avoid

Do not use:

```text
24/7 Emergency Vet
Always Open
Guaranteed Same-Day Appointment
Immediate Treatment
Emergency Surgery
Best Vet In Edmonds
#1 Vet
Cheapest Vet
Lowest Cost Vet
```

Unless legally and operationally verified.

## Message Match Rules

Ad copy should match the landing page.

If the ad says:

```text
Trusted Vet Care in Edmonds
```

the landing page hero should say the same or very similar.

If the ad says:

```text
Book A Vet Visit Today
```

the page must have a visible booking CTA above the fold.

---

## Compliance / Claim Rules

Do not imply:

- 24/7 care
- emergency hospital status
- guaranteed same-day appointment
- cheapest care
- best or #1 unless verified
- specialist services unless confirmed

---

## Performance Review Rules

After launch, evaluate:

- CTR by ad group
- Search-term quality
- Call clicks
- Booking clicks
- Form submissions
- Cost per lead
- Ad asset combinations if enough data exists

Do not rewrite ads from one day of data unless there is a compliance issue or obvious mismatch.

---

## Final Rule

General Vet ad copy should drive calm, local, appointment-focused intent.

Urgent or emergency language belongs in the urgent campaign, not this campaign.
