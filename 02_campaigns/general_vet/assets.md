# Assets - General Vet Campaign

## Campaign

Search - General Vet - Edmonds

## Purpose

This file defines campaign assets for the General Vet campaign.

Assets should support local trust, booking intent, phone calls, and service clarity.

They should not be emergency-first. Emergency-focused assets belong in the urgent campaign.

---

## Required Asset Types

The General Vet campaign should use:

```text
Call asset
Sitelinks
Callouts
Structured snippets
Location asset
```

Optional later:

```text
Image assets
Business name
Logo
Lead form asset
Promotion asset
```

---

## Call Asset

Use:

```text
(425) 673-0700
```

Phone link standard:

```text
tel:+14256730700
```

### Rules

- Must match landing page phone number.
- Must match website header/footer phone number.
- Must be campaign-eligible.
- Do not use any other phone number.

## Sitelinks

Add campaign-level sitelinks.

Do not rely on paused account-level Google AI sitelinks.

Recommended sitelinks:

| Sitelink Text | Description Line 1 | Description Line 2 | Final URL |
|---|---|---|---|
| Book Appointment | Schedule your pet visit | New patients welcome | Booking URL |
| New Patients | Visit Angel Pet Hospital | Dog and cat care in Edmonds | `/veterinarian-edmonds#new-patients` |
| Our Services | Vet care for dogs and cats | Wellness, sick visits and more | `/veterinarian-edmonds#services` |
| Get Directions | Find our Edmonds clinic | Visit Angel Pet Hospital | Google Maps directions URL |
| Contact Us | Call or request a visit | We are here to help | `/veterinarian-edmonds#contact` |
| Dog & Cat Care | Local care for pets | Book a vet appointment | `/veterinarian-edmonds#dog-cat-care` |

---

## Sitelink URL Rules

Use unique URLs when possible.

Recommended anchors to add to the landing page:

```text
#new-patients
#services
#contact
#dog-cat-care
#location
#book
```

If anchors do not exist yet, add them in the website repo before using anchor sitelinks.

Do not create duplicate sitelinks with the same text and same final URL.

## Lead Form Asset

Do not add Google lead form asset at launch unless strategy changes.

Reason:

The preferred lead paths are:

```text
phone call
booking
Zoho form / website form
```

Lead forms can create low-quality leads if not managed carefully.

## Performance Review Rules

After launch, review:

- asset impressions
- clicks
- CTR
- conversions
- disapprovals
- low-quality asset text
- duplicate sitelinks
- Google AI auto-created assets

Do not judge asset performance from one day of data.

---

## Final Rule

Assets should make the ad more useful and more trustworthy.

They should help users book, call, find the clinic, or understand services.
