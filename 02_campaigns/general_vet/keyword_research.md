# Keyword Research - General Vet Campaign

## Campaign

Search - General Vet - Edmonds

## Purpose

This file stores keyword research and keyword decisions for the future General Vet campaign.

The campaign should capture general local veterinary demand without mixing urgent-only, brand, dental, surgery, wellness, or promotion-specific intent.

This campaign should not reuse the old Campaign #1 structure.

## Campaign Intent

The General Vet campaign should target local pet owners searching for:

- a regular veterinarian
- an animal hospital
- a veterinary clinic
- a pet hospital
- dog vet care
- cat vet care
- new patient appointments
- routine veterinary appointments
- sick pet visits that are not urgent/emergency searches

---

## What This Campaign Should Not Capture

The General Vet campaign should not be used for:

- urgent vet searches
- emergency vet searches
- open-now emergency searches
- brand searches
- dental-specific searches
- surgery-specific searches
- wellness/vaccine-specific searches
- shelter/adoption/rescue/foster searches
- grooming/boarding searches
- job/career/training searches
- far-location searches
- competitor searches unless a competitor strategy is approved

## Legacy Campaign #1 Learnings - 2026-05-06

Campaign #1 was temporarily re-enabled because of concern that the new urgent campaign might not receive enough clicks.

After Codex QA, Campaign #1 was paused again.

This data is considered dirty legacy data. It should be used for research only, not copied as campaign structure.

Codex found Campaign #1 was structurally unsafe because Display/content was on, no Global Vet Waste Negatives were applied, bidding used Maximize Conversions with tCPA, the final URL pointed to `business.google.com`, and it generated competitor, shelter, far-location, and weak general-vet traffic. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

## Recommended Launch Ad Groups

Launch with a controlled structure.

Because starting budget is expected to be around $30/day, avoid too many ad groups at launch.

Recommended launch structure:

| Ad Group | Intent | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Vet / Veterinarian Near Me | Core general demand | Highest priority |
| Animal Hospital / Veterinary Clinic | Clinic/hospital demand | Strong local service intent |
| Dog & Cat Vet | Pet-type-specific demand | Use if volume supports it |
| Local City Vet | Geo-modified intent | Use carefully, focused on nearby cities only |

Do not start with too many ad groups because the budget may become too thin.

General Vet should use the Global Vet Waste Negatives list and campaign-specific negatives.

## Strategic Decision

Do not reuse Campaign #1.

Build General Vet as a new controlled Search campaign with:

- Search only
- Exact and Phrase match only
- dedicated `/veterinarian-edmonds` landing page
- Global Vet Waste Negatives
- campaign-specific urgent negatives
- campaign-specific competitor and far-location negatives
- Maximize Clicks at launch
- no Display
- no Search Partners
- no Broad Match
- no Target CPA
- no Maximize Conversions until tracking is mature
