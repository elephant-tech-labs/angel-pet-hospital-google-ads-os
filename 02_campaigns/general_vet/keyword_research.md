# Keyword Research - General Vet Campaign

## Campaign

Search - General Vet - Edmonds

## Purpose

This file stores keyword research and final keyword decisions for the General Vet campaign.

The campaign should capture general local veterinary demand without mixing into urgent-only, brand, dental, surgery, or wellness-specific campaign intent.
