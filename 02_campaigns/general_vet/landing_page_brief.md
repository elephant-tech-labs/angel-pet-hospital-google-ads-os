## Purpose

Create a dedicated landing page for general veterinary searches.

This page should convert local pet owners looking for a trusted veterinarian, animal hospital, or pet clinic in Edmonds.

This page is different from the urgent-care page.

## Design Direction

The page should feel:

- trustworthy
- warm
- professional
- local
- easy to book
- calm
- clean
- mobile-first

Avoid:

- generic stock-heavy design
- clutter
- long paragraphs
- weak CTAs
- homepage-style navigation overload
