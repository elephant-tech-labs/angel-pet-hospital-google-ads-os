# Research Brief - General Vet Campaign

## Campaign

Search - General Vet - Edmonds

## Purpose

Research and prepare a Google Search campaign for general veterinary care and new patient acquisition for Angel Pet Hospital in Edmonds, WA.

This campaign is not for urgent-only traffic. It should capture local pet owners looking for a trusted veterinarian, animal hospital, dog vet, cat vet, or veterinary clinic.

---

## Business Goal

Generate qualified new patient calls, appointment requests, online bookings, and form leads.

The campaign should support ongoing clinic growth, not just urgent care.

---

## Target Audience

Local pet owners in and around Edmonds who are looking for:

- a regular veterinarian
- a nearby animal hospital
- dog or cat veterinary care
- a new vet clinic
- routine or sick pet appointments
- wellness visits
- vaccinations
- general pet care

## Intended Campaign Positioning

Core message:

```text
Trusted Vet Care in Edmonds
```

Supporting message:

```text
Book veterinary care for dogs and cats with Angel Pet Hospital.
```

CTA hierarchy:

1. Book Appointment
2. Call Now
3. Request Call Back

This differs from the urgent campaign, where Call Now is primary.

---

## Research Questions

Codex or the research agent should answer:

1. What general vet search terms have appeared in existing Google Ads data?
2. Which terms from the urgent campaign should be moved to General Vet later?
3. Which terms should remain in Urgent Vet?
4. Which general vet keywords should be approved?
5. Which terms are too broad or risky?
6. Which negative keywords are needed?
7. Are any current negatives likely to block good general vet traffic?
8. What ad groups should be created?
9. What landing page is needed?
10. What budget and launch conditions are recommended?
11. Is the account ready to launch this campaign now?
12. What should be watched after launch?

---

## Research Sources

Use these sources if available:

1. Google Ads MCP live account data
2. Search terms from existing campaigns
3. Search keyword data
4. Negative keyword lists
