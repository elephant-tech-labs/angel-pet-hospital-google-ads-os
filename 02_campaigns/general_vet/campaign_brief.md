# Campaign Brief - Search - General Vet - Edmonds

## Campaign Name

Search - General Vet - Edmonds

## Purpose

Capture local pet owners searching for a regular veterinarian, animal hospital, or veterinary clinic near Edmonds.

This campaign is for general appointment demand, not urgent-only demand.

---

## Primary Goal

Generate qualified new patient calls, appointment requests, and online bookings.

---

## Secondary Goals

- Website call clicks
- Zoho form submissions
- Booking clicks
- Contact inquiries

---

## Campaign Intent

This campaign should target users searching for:

```text
vet near me
veterinarian near me
animal hospital near me
pet hospital near me
veterinary clinic near me
dog vet near me
cat vet near me
local vet
best vet near me
```

---

## How This Differs From Urgent Campaign

Urgent campaign:

```text
urgent vet
sick pet
open now
same day
emergency-adjacent
```

General Vet campaign:

```text
routine vet
new patient vet
dog and cat vet
local veterinarian
animal hospital
wellness and sick visits
```

Do not mix these campaigns heavily.

---

## Starting Budget

Recommended starting budget:

```text
$30/day
```

Only launch if urgent campaign is stable enough and total budget allows.

---

## Bidding

Recommended launch bidding:

```text
Maximize Clicks
```

Do not use Target CPA until tracking is clean and enough real lead conversions exist.

---

## Network Settings

Use:

```text
Google Search only
```

Must remain off:

```text
Search Partners
Display Network
AI Max / Final URL Expansion
```

---

## Match Types

Allowed:

```text
Exact
Phrase
```

Not allowed during early phase:

```text
Broad
```

---

## Landing Page Needed

Recommended landing page:

```text
https://www.angelpethospital.com/veterinarian-edmonds
```

If the page does not exist yet, build it before launch.

Do not send this campaign to the homepage long-term.

---

## CTA Hierarchy

Primary:

```text
Book Appointment
Call Now
```

Secondary:

```text
Request Call Back
Contact Us
```

For general vet traffic, booking can be more prominent than on urgent traffic.

---

## Suggested Ad Positioning

Core message:

```text
Trusted Vet Care in Edmonds
```

Support message:

```text
Book veterinary care for dogs and cats with Angel Pet Hospital.
```

---

## Suggested Keyword Themes

Ad groups should be separated by theme:

1. Vet Near Me
2. Animal Hospital
3. Dog and Cat Vet
4. New Patient Vet
5. Local Veterinarian

---

## Negative Keyword Rules

Block:

```text
adoption
shelter
rescue
foster
jobs
career
school
training
grooming
boarding
free
cheap
low cost
competitor terms
far cities
```

Watch:

```text
emergency
urgent
open now
```

If urgent terms appear, move those searches to the urgent campaign.

---

## Launch Requirements

Before launch:

- Landing page exists.
- Phone number is correct.
- Booking CTA works.
- Call CTA works.
- Negative list is ready.
- Tracking is configured or at least planned.
- Search Partners off.
- Display off.
- Broad match not used.

---

## Success Metrics

Primary:

- qualified calls
- appointment requests
- booking completions
- form submissions
- cost per qualified lead

Secondary:

- CTR
- CPC
- search term quality
- location quality
- landing page conversion rate

---

## Do Not Launch If

Do not launch this campaign if:

- urgent campaign is still leaking waste heavily
- tracking is completely broken
- landing page is not ready
- total budget is too thin
- clinic cannot handle additional appointment volume
