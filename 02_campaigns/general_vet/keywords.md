# Keywords - General Vet Campaign

## Campaign

Search - General Vet - Edmonds

## Purpose

This file defines the approved keyword system for the General Vet campaign.

The campaign should capture nearby pet owners looking for a regular veterinarian, animal hospital, veterinary clinic, dog vet, cat vet, or general pet care appointment.

This campaign should not capture urgent-only, emergency-only, brand, dental, surgery, wellness-only, shelter/adoption, grooming, boarding, job seeker, or far-location searches.

---

## Campaign Intent

The General Vet campaign targets local users searching for:

- vet near me
- veterinarian near me
- animal hospital near me
- pet hospital near me
- veterinary clinic near me
- dog vet near me
- cat vet near me
- local vet in Edmonds
- vet appointments
- new patient veterinary care

---

## Approved Match Type Strategy

Use only:

```text
Exact
Phrase
```

Do not use:

```text
Broad Match
Dynamic Search Ads
AI keyword expansion
Auto-applied keyword suggestions
```

---

## Launch Ad Group Structure

Because the starting budget is limited, do not launch too many ad groups.

Approved launch structure:

```text
1. Vet / Veterinarian Near Me
2. Animal Hospital / Veterinary Clinic
3. Dog & Cat Vet
4. Local City Vet
```

---

## Ad Group 1: Vet / Veterinarian Near Me

### Purpose

Capture the core general-vet demand from local pet owners searching for a nearby veterinarian.

### Keywords

```text
"vet near me"
[vet near me]
"veterinarian near me"
[veterinarian near me]
"local vet near me"
"best vet near me"
```

### Notes

This ad group is broad but important.

Watch search terms closely because “vet near me” can pull:

- emergency intent
- low-cost intent
- competitor intent
- far-location intent
- job/research intent

---

## Ad Group 2: Animal Hospital / Veterinary Clinic

### Purpose

Capture users searching for a clinic, animal hospital, or pet hospital rather than just the word “vet.”

### Keywords

```text
"animal hospital near me"
[animal hospital near me]
"pet hospital near me"
[pet hospital near me]
"veterinary clinic near me"
[veterinary clinic near me]
"vet clinic near me"
```

### Notes

This is a strong general-vet intent group.

Watch for competitor names and emergency hospital intent.

---

## Ad Group 3: Dog & Cat Vet

### Purpose

Capture pet-type-specific searches from dog and cat owners.

### Keywords

```text
"dog vet near me"
"dog veterinarian near me"
"cat vet near me"
"cat veterinarian near me"
"vet for dogs and cats"
```

### Notes

This ad group can help improve message match for pet owners.

Watch volume. If volume is low, this ad group can stay small.

---

## Ad Group 4: Local City Vet

### Purpose

Capture local geo-modified searches in Edmonds and nearby communities.

### Keywords

```text
"edmonds vet"
[edmonds vet]
"edmonds veterinarian"
"veterinarian edmonds"
"animal hospital edmonds"
"vet clinic lynnwood"
"lynnwood vet"
"mountlake terrace vet"
"shoreline vet"
```

### Notes

Use carefully.

Core focus should remain Edmonds and nearby communities.

Watch these especially:

```text
"shoreline vet"
"lynnwood vet"
"mountlake terrace vet"
```

If traffic quality is weak or too far, tighten.

These are approved only with close monitoring:

"best vet near me"
"animal clinic near me"
"pet clinic near me"
"veterinary hospital near me"
"vet clinic lynnwood"
"shoreline vet"

Reasons:

- may be broad
- may trigger competitor traffic
- may trigger far-location traffic
- may overlap with urgent campaign
- may need tighter negatives
