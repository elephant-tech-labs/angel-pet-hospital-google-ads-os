# Google Ads Decision Rules

## Purpose

These rules prevent random or premature campaign changes.

## Do Not Change Without Approval

- Bidding strategy
- Daily budget
- Campaign type
- Network settings
- Match type strategy
- Conversion goals
- Location radius
- Ad claims
- Landing page final URL
- Campaign status
- Brand/non-brand separation

## Not Allowed In Early Phase

- Broad match
- Performance Max
- Display prospecting
- Search Partners
- Target CPA
- Maximize Conversions with weak tracking
- Auto-apply Google recommendations
- Aggressive keyword expansion

## Smart Bidding Rule

Do not use Target CPA or Maximize Conversions until:
- conversion tracking is clean
- phone calls are tracked
- website call clicks are tracked
- form/booking tracking is working
- weak conversions are not primary
- enough qualified leads exist

## Campaign Expansion Rule

Only launch new campaigns when:
- current campaign is stable
- tracking is not broken
- landing page exists or is planned
- budget is not spread too thin
- search intent is clearly separated

## Scale Rule

Increase budget only when:
- search terms are clean
- leads are real
- clinic confirms lead quality
- cost per qualified lead is acceptable
- no major competitor or far-city leakage exists

## Pause / Fix Rule

Take action when:
- competitor clicks spend money
- far-city clicks spend money
- shelter/adoption/free/jobs/grooming/boarding terms appear
- calls happen outside answering hours
- CPC spikes without lead quality
- landing page CTA breaks
- phone numbers mismatch

## Urgent Campaign Keyword Rules

Allowed:
- urgent vet
- sick pet vet
- vet open now
- animal hospital open now
- same-day vet
- emergency-adjacent terms

Watch:
- emergency vet
- emergency pet care
- vet near me
- animal hospital near me

Move later:
- general vet terms
- dog vet near me
- cat vet near me
- veterinary clinic near me

## Unsafe Ad Claims Unless Confirmed

Do not use:
- 24/7 Emergency Vet
- Guaranteed Same-Day Appointment
- Immediate Emergency Treatment
- Emergency Surgery
- Always Open
