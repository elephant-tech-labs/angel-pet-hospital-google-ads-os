## Strategic Principles

1. Lead quality matters more than click volume.
2. Brand and non-brand traffic must be separated.
3. Emergency, urgent, general, service, and brand intent must not be mixed.
4. Search-only campaigns come before automation.
5. Homepage should not be the default paid traffic destination.
6. Dedicated landing pages should be used for campaign intent.
7. Conversion tracking must focus on real leads, not page views.
8. Google recommendations are not strategy.
9. Do not scale budget before lead quality is proven.
10. Do not use Broad Match, PMax, Display, or Target CPA until the account is ready.
