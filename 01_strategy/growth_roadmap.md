# Google Ads Growth Roadmap

## Objective

Build a scalable Google Ads acquisition system for Angel Pet Hospital that increases qualified client intake while reducing wasted spend.

## Phase 1: Stabilization

### Goal
Stop waste and prove urgent-intent traffic quality.

### Active Campaign
Search - Urgent Vet Care - Edmonds

### Focus
- Search-only traffic
- Exact/Phrase keywords
- Strong negative keyword control
- Dedicated urgent landing page
- Call-focused CTA
- Daily search term review
- Tracking setup

### Do Not Do Yet
- Broad Match
- Performance Max
- Display Network
- Search Partners
- Target CPA
- Budget increase
- Major keyword expansion

## Phase 2: General Vet Campaign

### Campaign
Search - General Vet - Edmonds

### Goal
Capture regular new-patient demand.

### Intent
- vet near me
- animal hospital near me
- veterinarian near me
- dog vet near me
- cat vet near me
- veterinary clinic near me

### Landing Page Needed
/veterinarian-edmonds

### Budget
Start around $30/day after urgent campaign is stable.

## Phase 3: Brand Campaign

### Campaign
Search - Brand - Angel Pet Hospital

### Goal
Protect brand searches and control messaging.

### Budget
$5-$10/day initially.

### Keywords
- angel pet hospital
- angel pet hospital edmonds
- angel pet hospital wa

## Phase 4: High-Value Service Campaigns

### Dental
Search - Pet Dental Care - Edmonds

### Surgery
Search - Veterinary Surgery - Edmonds

### Wellness / Vaccines
Search - Wellness & Vaccines - Edmonds

### Rule
Only launch service campaigns with proper landing pages and conversion tracking.

## Phase 5: Offers / Promotions

Possible campaigns:
- New client exam offer
- Dental month promotion
- Senior pet wellness package
- Puppy/kitten first visit
- Vaccine reminder campaign

Must confirm:
- offer economics
- clinic capacity
- margin
- operational approval

## Phase 6: Remarketing

Only after tracking is installed.

Audiences:
- urgent page visitors
- booking visitors
- form starters
- dental page visitors
- general vet page visitors

## Phase 7: Performance Max

Only consider after:
- Search campaigns have stable qualified lead volume
- Conversion tracking is clean
- Offline/qualified lead feedback exists
- Strong landing pages exist
- Exclusions and brand controls are in place

Performance Max should not be used as a first-stage lead-gen shortcut.
