# Account Strategy - Angel Pet Hospital Google Ads

## Business Goal

Angel Pet Hospital wants to increase qualified client intake through Google Ads.

The main goal is not traffic volume. The main goal is more qualified:
- phone calls
- appointment requests
- online bookings
- Zoho form leads
- booked appointments
- new patients

## Strategic Direction

Build a controlled, intent-segmented Google Ads system.

The account should eventually separate campaigns by user intent:

1. Urgent Vet Care
2. General Vet / New Patient
3. Brand
4. Dental
5. Surgery
6. Wellness / Vaccines
7. Senior Pet Care
8. Puppy / Kitten Care
9. Promotional / seasonal campaigns
10. Remarketing
11. Performance Max only after tracking and lead quality are mature

## Current Phase

Phase 1: Stabilization and urgent-care lead capture.

The active focus is the campaign:

Search - Urgent Vet Care - Edmonds

This campaign is designed to capture high-intent local searches from pet owners looking for urgent vet help, same-day sick pet visits, open-now vet care, or emergency-adjacent searches.

## Strategic Rules

- Search-only first.
- Exact and Phrase match only during early phase.
- Dedicated landing pages for paid traffic.
- No homepage as the default final URL for campaign-specific traffic.
- No Broad Match until tracking, negatives, and lead quality are mature.
- No Performance Max until Search campaigns prove clean lead quality.
- No Display Network prospecting during early phase.
- No Search Partners during stabilization.
- No Target CPA until conversion tracking is clean and enough qualified leads exist.
- No aggressive scaling until lead quality is proven.

## Lead Quality Definition

A high-quality lead is one of:

1. Phone call from a nearby pet owner with a real care need.
2. Website call click from the urgent landing page.
3. Appointment booking or booking request.
4. Zoho form submission with valid contact information.
5. Confirmed booked appointment from clinic feedback.

Weak actions are not true leads:
- Page views
- Scrolls
- Generic engagement
- Directions clicks
- Contact page visits
- Low-duration or wrong-number calls

## Budget Strategy

Current starting budget:
- Urgent Vet Care: $70/day
- Future General Vet: $30/day

Do not increase budget until search terms, calls, and tracking quality are reviewed.

## Success Metrics

Primary:
- qualified calls
- form submissions
- appointment requests
- booked appointments
- cost per qualified lead

Secondary:
- CTR
- CPC
- search term quality
- impression share
- landing page conversion rate
- location quality
- device quality

Clicks alone are not success.
